# E-E-A-T Evaluation Checklist

Complete checklist for evaluating Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness signals.

---

## Experience (First-hand knowledge)

### Content Signals
- [ ] First-person accounts ("In my experience...", "When I...")
- [ ] Specific examples from direct involvement
- [ ] Photos/videos showing hands-on work
- [ ] Case studies with detailed process descriptions
- [ ] Before/after examples with commentary
- [ ] "Lessons learned" from real projects

### Author Signals
- [ ] Author has worked in the field
- [ ] Portfolio of relevant work
- [ ] Demonstrated use of products/services reviewed
- [ ] Personal anecdotes that show real experience

### Page Elements
- [ ] Original photos (not stock images)
- [ ] Screenshots from actual use
- [ ] Documented processes with real examples
- [ ] User-generated content showing experience

---

## Expertise (Knowledge and skill)

### Author Credentials
- [ ] Relevant degree or certification (PhD, MD, CPA, etc.)
- [ ] Professional licenses mentioned
- [ ] Years of experience stated
- [ ] Job title/position relevant to content
- [ ] Published works or research
- [ ] Speaking engagements listed

### Content Quality
- [ ] Technical accuracy
- [ ] Comprehensive coverage of topic
- [ ] Current, up-to-date information
- [ ] Proper citations and references
- [ ] Appropriate depth for audience
- [ ] Correct use of terminology

### Author Pages
- [ ] Dedicated author bio page
- [ ] Photo of author
- [ ] Links to professional profiles (LinkedIn)
- [ ] List of credentials and qualifications
- [ ] Link to author's other work
- [ ] Contact information

### External Validation
- [ ] Author cited by others
- [ ] Guest posts on authoritative sites
- [ ] Quoted in news articles
- [ ] Academic citations

---

## Authoritativeness (Recognition and reputation)

### Site-Level Authority
- [ ] Well-known brand in the industry
- [ ] Mentioned by other authoritative sources
- [ ] Cited by news outlets
- [ ] Referenced in Wikipedia
- [ ] Appears in Google Knowledge Graph
- [ ] High-quality backlinks from relevant sites

### Industry Recognition
- [ ] Industry awards displayed
- [ ] Certifications and accreditations
- [ ] Membership in professional organizations
- [ ] Partnerships with recognized brands
- [ ] Media features and press coverage
- [ ] Case studies with notable clients

### Social Proof
- [ ] Testimonials from recognizable sources
- [ ] Reviews on third-party platforms
- [ ] Social media following
- [ ] Engagement from industry peers
- [ ] Speaking at industry events

### Content Authority
- [ ] Original research cited by others
- [ ] Definitive guides in the space
- [ ] First to cover important topics
- [ ] Subject matter experts contribute

---

## Trustworthiness (Reliability and honesty)

### Transparency
- [ ] Clear "About Us" page
- [ ] Physical address listed
- [ ] Phone number available
- [ ] Email contact provided
- [ ] Team/leadership pages
- [ ] Company history disclosed

### Legal/Privacy
- [ ] Privacy policy linked
- [ ] Terms of service available
- [ ] Cookie consent implemented
- [ ] GDPR compliance (if applicable)
- [ ] Disclosure of affiliate relationships
- [ ] Editorial policy stated

### Security
- [ ] HTTPS enabled
- [ ] Secure payment processing
- [ ] Trust seals displayed
- [ ] No malware warnings
- [ ] Clean URL structure

### Content Integrity
- [ ] Fact-checked content
- [ ] Corrections published when needed
- [ ] Sources cited
- [ ] No misleading headlines
- [ ] Balanced viewpoints presented
- [ ] Clear distinction between ads and content

### User Safety
- [ ] No dark patterns
- [ ] Clear refund policies
- [ ] Customer support accessible
- [ ] User reviews visible (good and bad)
- [ ] Data handling explained

---

## YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) Additional Requirements

For sites covering health, finance, safety, or other high-stakes topics:

### Health/Medical Content
- [ ] Medical reviewer credited
- [ ] Sources from peer-reviewed research
- [ ] Last reviewed date shown
- [ ] Disclaimer about medical advice
- [ ] Author has medical credentials

### Financial Content
- [ ] Financial credentials stated (CFA, CFP, CPA)
- [ ] Disclosure of potential conflicts
- [ ] Regulatory compliance noted
- [ ] Risk warnings included
- [ ] Not promising unrealistic returns

### Legal Content
- [ ] Attorney credentials verified
- [ ] Jurisdiction specified
- [ ] Disclaimer about legal advice
- [ ] Licensed bar membership

### Safety/Emergency Content
- [ ] Verified by experts
- [ ] Emergency contact information
- [ ] Clear warnings and disclaimers
- [ ] Updated regularly

## Scoring Guide

### Experience Score (5 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Multiple clear demonstrations of hands-on experience |
| 4 | Good evidence of direct experience |
| 3 | Some experience indicators |
| 2 | Limited experience signals |
| 1 | No visible experience |

### Expertise Score (5 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Strong credentials, comprehensive author pages |
| 4 | Good credentials, some validation |
| 3 | Basic credentials present |
| 2 | Limited expertise signals |
| 1 | No expertise indicators |

### Authoritativeness Score (5 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Industry leader, widely recognized |
| 4 | Well-known in niche, good backlinks |
| 3 | Some recognition and citations |
| 2 | Limited authority signals |
| 1 | No authority indicators |

### Trustworthiness Score (5 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Full transparency, all policies present, secure |
| 4 | Good transparency, most elements present |
| 3 | Basic trust elements |
| 2 | Missing key trust signals |
| 1 | Major trust concerns |

4. Display certifications and awards
5. Create detailed team page
